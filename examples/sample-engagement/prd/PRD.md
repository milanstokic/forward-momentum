## Objective

This release revamps the Forkful checkout to reduce drop-off.

- Checkout has a ~31% drop-off between add-to-cart and order-placed, and reducing it is the Q3 priority. [claim-001 · sources/kickoff-call.md:L9]
- Most of that drop-off is concentrated at the payment step. [claim-003 · sources/product-notes.md:L7]

## Background / Problem

- Declined-payment handling is broken today: on a decline the user just sees the form again with no message, retries, and rage-quits. [claim-010 · sources/kickoff-call.md:L35]
- The current flow is four screens; the revamp aims to collapse it. [claim-005 · sources/product-notes.md:L10-L11]

## Goals & Success Metrics

- Reduce checkout drop-off from 31% to under 20% this release. [claim-002 · sources/product-notes.md:L6]
- The new declined-payment experience must feel less broken than today's silent failure. [claim-010 · sources/kickoff-call.md:L35]

## Users

- Forkful shoppers completing a food order; the flow targets both first-time and returning buyers. [claim-004 · sources/kickoff-call.md:L19]

## Scope (what we're building)

- A single-page checkout flow: cart summary → delivery address → payment → place order. [claim-005 · sources/kickoff-call.md:L23]
- Payment supports Apple Pay and Google Pay alongside the existing manual card form. [claim-006 · sources/product-notes.md:L12]
- On mobile, wallets are the primary, most-prominent payment option. [claim-007 · sources/product-notes.md:L24]
- A confirmation screen after order placement shows the order number, estimated delivery time, and a "we're on it" message. [claim-011 · sources/kickoff-call.md:L37]
- The cart summary shows a delivery ETA that recalculates when the address changes. [claim-015 · sources/product-notes.md:L20]
- Loyalty points accrue at 1 point per dollar on every completed order, surfaced after the order completes. [claim-016 · sources/product-notes.md:L27-L28]
- Promo / discount codes (e.g. "first order 20% off") must continue to work in the new flow. [claim-012 · sources/product-notes.md:L35]
- The happy-path frames are mocked: cart summary, delivery address, payment (wallets), payment (card form), and the place-order CTA state. [claim-017 · sources/design-references.md:L12-L16]

## Key Decisions

- **Account model (conflict-001 resolved):** every order is tied to a signed-in Forkful account, but signup is reduced to a lightweight inline step folded into the address step — there is no separate signup wall. [conflict-001 · decisions/2026-06-13-resolution-conflict-001.md]
- This resolves the contradiction between the call's "guest checkout, no account" and the notes' "every order tied to a signed-in account" in favor of account correctness (loyalty ledger, chargebacks) while preserving low friction. [claim-009, claim-004 · decisions/2026-06-13-resolution-conflict-001.md]

## Out of Scope / Parked

- Cart recovery / abandoned-cart email is explicitly parked and not part of this release. [claim-013 · sources/kickoff-call.md:L45]
- Broad "don't lose the cart" in-session work was capped with "let's not boil the ocean"; focus stays on the checkout itself. [claim-014 · sources/kickoff-call.md:L43]
- Only the happy path is mocked so far; the back half of the flow is not yet mocked. [claim-018 · sources/design-references.md:L23]

## Open Questions (non-blocking gaps still pending)

- Saved-card behavior, and how it interacts with the account/inline-signup model, is parked and undefined — must be settled before the payment step is built. [gap-001 · sources/kickoff-call.md:L31]
- No design frame exists for the declined / failed-payment error state, despite it being a named pain point. [gap-002 · sources/design-references.md:L23]
- The order-confirmation screen is described as "the payoff" but is missing from the design references. [gap-003 · sources/design-references.md:L16]
- Promo-code handling is acknowledged but unspecified: where it is entered, how it is validated, whether codes stack, and how it affects the total. [gap-004 · sources/kickoff-call.md:L49]
- In-session cart persistence for the new single-page flow is undefined: whether the cart survives a refresh or navigation away mid-checkout. [gap-005 · sources/kickoff-call.md:L43]
